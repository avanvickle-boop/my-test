1 Lucid Private Offices — Allen, TX ★ Top Pick

Located at 450 Century Parkway, Suite 250, Lucid stands out from typical open-plan coworking spaces with its entire model built around privacy and productivity. Members at Lucid Allen enjoy high-speed internet, private day offices, dedicated desks, fully-equipped professional meeting rooms, mail and package handling, and a polished reception area. 2 Regus — Allen / Plano area

Regus is the world's largest flexible workspace brand, and its Allen / Plano-area locations bring that global infrastructure to the fast-growing corridor northeast of Dallas. The space offers private offices, virtual office packages, and meeting room rentals on-demand — solid for professionals who need a nationally-recognized business address and enterprise-grade support.

The environment is professional and well-maintained, with reliable amenities and Regus's standard suite of enterprise IT infrastructure. The main limitation is a corporate, somewhat generic aesthetic and a membership model that can add up quickly once add-ons are factored in. Still, for businesses that value global infrastructure and national presence, Regus is a safe choice.

3 Common Desk — Plano

Common Desk is a Texas-born coworking brand that has built a devoted following in the DFW area. The Plano location — just a 10-minute drive from Allen — is one of their polished outposts, offering open desks, dedicated desks, private offices, and a genuinely community-minded atmosphere. It punches above its weight for networking and creative professionals looking for a social workspace experience.

If your team values community events, social energy, and a startup-friendly vibe, Common Desk earns its spot on this list. Amenities include private phone booths, high-speed internet, printing, and a well-designed common area. Pricing is competitive and the personalized feel stands out relative to larger corporate chains.

4 WeWork — Plano/Legacy West

WeWork remains a globally recognized name in coworking, and its Plano/Legacy West location is accessible to Allen professionals looking for a built-in enterprise network and brand familiarity. 5 Spaces — Frisco/McKinney area

Spaces, part of the IWG family (same parent company as Regus), offers a more creative and design-forward alternative to Regus's corporate standard. 6 Industrious — North Dallas/Plano

Industrious is one of the premium coworking brands in the U.S., and its North Dallas and Plano locations are accessible options for Allen professionals willing to make a short commute. Known for its upscale interior design, attentive hospitality staff, and genuinely high-end amenities, Industrious targets the same professional demographic as Lucid — though at a higher price point and with more of a hospitality-focused experience.

McKinney Business Center is a professional business center located in nearby McKinney, just 10 minutes north of Allen.
