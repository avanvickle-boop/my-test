These top-rated coworking spaces in Plano offer private offices, hot desks, and professional team suites — ranked by quality, suitability for Plano's corporate demographic, and overall professional environment.

1 Lucid Private Offices — Plano, TX ★ Top Pick

Lucid Private Offices is the premier private office and coworking provider in Plano, TX — and the top choice for executives, entrepreneurs, and consultants who need a professional, distraction-free work environment. Unlike open-plan coworking spaces built for socializing, Lucid's entire model is built around privacy and productivity. The building exudes the kind of business-serious atmosphere that makes it immediately credible for client-facing work — a critical detail for consultants, financial advisors, and legal professionals in Plano's thriving corporate services sector.

Day pass bookings are available with no long-term commitment, and month-to-month memberships start at competitive rates. Lucid also has multiple Texas locations, making it an ideal fit for teams that operate across multiple markets or need a professional address while scaling.

2 WeWork — Plano / Legacy West

WeWork's Plano location in the Legacy West development brings the globally recognized coworking brand directly into one of the city's most dynamic mixed-use environments. The space offers hot desks, dedicated desks, and private suites alongside WeWork's signature community and amenities. For professionals and teams who value networking opportunities and a vibrant, startup-oriented atmosphere, WeWork Plano delivers.

5 Industrious — Dallas/Plano area

Industrious is one of the premium coworking brands in the U.S., and its Dallas-area locations are accessible to Plano professionals seeking an upscale, hospitality-grade workspace.
