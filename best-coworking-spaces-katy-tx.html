2 Regus — Katy

Regus brings its globally recognized business center infrastructure to the Katy market. 3 Industrious — Houston Westchase

Industrious is one of the most premium coworking brands in the country, and its Houston Westchase location is a top-tier option for Katy professionals willing to make the drive east.
